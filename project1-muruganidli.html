Murugan Idli Kadai

Murugan Idli Shop is a popular chain of South Indian restaurants that originated in Madurai and is now famous for its soft idlis, especially the ghee podi idli. The chain is known for its authentic South Indian cuisine, including other dishes like onion uthappam, various chutneys, and the popular cold drink Jigarthanda. It has expanded to include numerous outlets in Chennai and other cities, both within India and internationally.
